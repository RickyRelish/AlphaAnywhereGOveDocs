Nazareth Care Australasia

Welcome to Our Regional Intranet, a tool which will help you find accurate and up to date information that will enable you to carry out your role within Nazareth Care.

In the Policies & Processes section, you will find guiding principles for operating a business.

All supporting forms, templates and other documentation can be found in Documents & Resources

We hope that it will be a user-friendly way of helping you to have ready access to all our policies and procedures, forms, and other information. 

It will become the single place that all employees can go to, to find any information relevant to Nazareth Care and our work.

 We invite you to browse through the intranet, have a look at the information that is available, and feedback to us any thoughts, suggestions or ideas for improvement.
 
Thank you for being part of Nazareth Care. We value the skills and experience and knowledge that you bring to our organisation, 

and we hope that the Nazareth Care Document Management System will provide you with another way of enhancing your knowledge and professional development.
